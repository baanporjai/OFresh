FAQ

Got Questions?

Answers to the most common questions about O'Fresh

💰 How much does a cup cost?

69 Baht per cup (280 ml of fresh orange juice). Pay via QR Code or credit/debit card.

💳 What payment methods are accepted?

QR Code / PromptPay, TrueMoney, WeChat Pay, and VISA/Mastercard credit/debit cards. Google Wallet and Apple Pay are not supported.

⏱️ How long can I keep it?

Best consumed within 2 hours of squeezing for optimal taste and nutrition, as it contains no preservatives.

🍊 What type of oranges are used?

Premium-grade Taiwanese Mandarin oranges — sweet, juicy, and rich in Vitamin C. Stored at 3-9°C at all times.

🚫 Does it contain sugar or additives?

None at all — no added sugar, no ice, no preservatives, no artificial colors. 100% pure freshly squeezed juice.

⚙️ What if the machine has a problem?

Contact us at Tel. 087-986-7000 or LINE @ofresh. Our team is ready to help every day.
